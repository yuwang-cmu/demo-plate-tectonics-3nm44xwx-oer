# Plate Tectonics: A Systems View of a Dynamic Earth

This two-chapter public OER package demonstrates the structure and appearance of introductory undergraduate Earth-science materials. Chapter 1 treats plate tectonics as a connected explanatory system. Chapter 2 applies that system to earthquakes, volcanoes, tsunamis, monitoring, warning, and risk reduction.

This is an Alembic demonstration only and will not be used directly for a class. Its purpose is to show what an AI agent can produce through Coursewerk without human-authored lesson content or subject-matter editing; human involvement was limited to commissioning the demo, auditing the pipeline, checking consistency, and overseeing publication.

Each chapter includes a study guide, visual lecture slides, a concept map, assessment-design support, and a 15-item worked practice set. Raster field photographs are included locally; Alembic trial imports may require those raster files to be re-added after publication, while the SVG diagrams import as text assets.

Yu Wang is the accountable publisher and curator of this AI-generated demonstration.
